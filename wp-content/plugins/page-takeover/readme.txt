== Description ==

**Promote Your Content in a Full-Screen Popup!**

Page Takeover allows you to create a stunning full-screen popup for your blog or website, without the need to know any HTML or CSS. 

Simply install the plugin, pick your colors, change the texts and your call-to-action, and load a distraction-free full-screen popup on your website.

**Promote Anything!**

* Promote any post or page
* Promote any external URL
* Promote your recommended posts
* Promote your products or services
* Promote your special offers or coupon codes
* Promote your lead page or lead magnet
* Promote your podcast or webinar
* Promote your social media accounts

== Installation ==

1. Download and unzip the plugin.
2. Upload the `page-takeover` directory to the `wp-content/plugins/` directory.
3. Click on 'Plugins' in the navigation menu and activate the plugin.
4. Click on 'Page Takeover' in the navigation menu.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= I've created my full-screen overlay, but am unable to see it on my website. Why? =
 
Make sure your overlay is active. Change the setting under the tab Overlay Status. Also, please delete your browser cookies and try again.

= How can I get support? =
 
Having trouble with the plugin? Simply post your question to the [support forum](https://wordpress.org/support/plugin/page-takeover) and we'll help you out.
